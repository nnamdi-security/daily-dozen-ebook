## 📖 About the Book

*The Daily Dozen* is a practical, no-nonsense guide to building a healthier life one habit at a time. Rather than offering another generic wellness checklist, the book breaks down **twelve daily habits** — grouped into five chapters — and explains not just *what* to do, but *why* it matters and *how* to make each habit stick using simple, repeatable routines.

The twelve habits covered are:

`Water` · `Sun` · `Sleep` · `Move` · `Food` · `Calm` · `Nature` · `Breath` · `Gut` · `Gratitude` · `Stretch` · `Mind`

## 📚 Table of Contents

| Chapter | Focus |

Each chapter closes with a short reflection to reinforce the core theme before moving to the next.

## ✍️ About the Author

Peter N. Ileh is a naturopathic practitioner who has spent the last several years studying natural medicine and alternative approaches to wellness, testing many of the principles in this book on himself before sharing them with others. The book is written from personal experience rather than pure theory, with the goal of helping readers build a stronger, more resilient relationship with their own health.
